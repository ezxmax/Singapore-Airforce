Server Rules

  1. Respect Everyone: Treat others with kindness and respect.
  2. No Spamming or Flooding: Avoid excessive posting of messages, emojis, or images.
  3. Keep Content Appropriate: Ensure all shared content is suitable for all age groups.
  4. Use Channels Appropriately: Post content in the appropriate channels according to their assigned topics.
  5. No NSFW Content: Do not share explicit content.
  6. Respect Privacy: Protect your own privacy and the privacy of others.
  7. Follow Discord's Terms of Service: Adhere to Discord's guidelines at all times.
  8. Listen to Staff: Follow the instructions of server moderators and administrators.
  9. No Advertising or Self-Promotion: Do not advertise without permission.
  10. Reporting Issues: Report problems to server staff.
